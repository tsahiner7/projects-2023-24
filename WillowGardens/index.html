Welcome to Willow Gardens

Cultivating Community Through Willow Gardens

Beautiful gardens lie at the heart of every thriving community, offering spaces for recreation, connection, and a sense of belonging. In Wisconsin Dells, WI, the Willow Gardens initiative is transforming these green spaces into vibrant hubs for all community members to engage in gardening and sustainable living. This inclusive approach not only encourages individuals and families to grow their own food, positively impacting household food security, but also fosters healthier eating habits and a deeper appreciation for nature.

Building Bonds and Sharing Knowledge at Willow Gardens

Willow Gardens provides much more than just gardening opportunities; it creates a lively meeting place where people of all ages, incomes, and cultures can come together. This dynamic environment encourages the exchange of gardening knowledge, techniques, and ideas, leading to stronger community bonds and shared experiences. Gardeners are encouraged to take an active role in the garden's success through volunteer commitments for site maintenance and management. By working collaboratively, community members not only cultivate the land but also strengthen their relationships and promote a sense of unity in Wisconsin Dells.

What do you receive if you buy a plot?

  • Land to rent, starter seeds, and on-site water.
  • Community compost, garden tools, and wood chips when available.
  • Measured, marked, and monitored plots.
  • Mowed grass borders to prevent rodent habitat.
  • Teaching materials and advice from our community mentors.
